Core Features
1. Appointment Coordination

Distribute patients across doctors, specialties, and time slots
Prevent overcrowding at popular times while other slots stay empty
Balance load dynamically based on real demand, not fixed scheduling

2. Patient Routing

Route patients based on symptoms, priority level, patient category, and required services
Direct patients to the correct area from the first step
Eliminate repeated trips and wrong-queue errors

3. Wait-Time Estimation

Analyze number of patients waiting, average consultation time, and clinic/equipment status
Forecast expected service time for each patient
Display estimates via app, digital screen, or SMS

4. Service-Sequencing Recommendations

Auto-order tests and procedures (blood work, ultrasound, X-ray, CT, MRI, etc.)
Base sequencing on wait times, fasting requirements, result turnaround time, and equipment availability
Example: draw blood → X-ray while blood processes → ultrasound → return to doctor once all results are ready
Minimizes idle waiting and backtracking between departments

5. Real-Time Adjustment

Re-coordinate the plan when a clinic is overloaded, equipment fails, a doctor's schedule shifts, or an emergency case arrives
Keep all downstream steps (routing, sequencing, wait estimates) updated automatically
